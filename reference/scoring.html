Scoring & Severity ​

How the 100-point rubric maps to score bands and how the scorer assigns severity levels to findings.

Score bands ​

RangeLabelMeaning
90–100ExcellentProduction-ready; minor or no issues
80–89GoodSolid; one or two non-critical gaps
70–79AdequateMeets threshold; noticeable gaps to address
60–69WeakBelow threshold; significant issues
<60RewriteFundamental problems; recommend rewriting from scratch

Default pass threshold: 70. Configurable in .claude/nlpm.local.md.

Severity levels ​

SeverityLocal scorerAuditor schema
HIGHpenalty ≥ 10 pointsscorer reproduced the breakage during the scoring pass; confidence: high, requires concrete evidence. Only HIGH findings reach the contribute step.
MEDIUMpenalty 5–9 pointsdefault confidence; no reproducer required.
LOWpenalty < 5 pointsweakest signal; kept for our own learning but not for PRs.
